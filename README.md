## 📜 社区宣言 (The Manifesto)

人类正在经历从**“信息互联网 (Information Internet)”**向**“意图互联网 (Intent Internet)”**的跨越。

Humanity is experiencing a leap from the **"Information Internet"** to the **"Intent Internet"**.

随着大语言模型（LLM）与具身智能（Embodied AI）的爆发，硅谷的前沿共识已逐渐将 LLM 视作下一代计算平台的“操作系统 CPU”。然而，现有的多智能体（Multi-Agent）研究大多停留在数字世界的文本生成与代码推演层面，**严重脱离了物理世界（Physical World）对确定性、合规性与极低容错率的硬性要求。**

With the explosion of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Embodied AI, the cutting-edge consensus in Silicon Valley has gradually regarded LLMs as the "Operating System CPU" of the next-generation computing platform. However, existing Multi-Agent research mostly stays at the level of text generation and code derivation in the digital world, **seriously detached from the hard requirements of the Physical World for certainty, compliance, and extremely low fault tolerance.**

**Nooxus-AI 团队**建立本开源研究社区，致力于回答一个终极命题：**当 AI 智能体跨越企业内网的防火墙、跨国界深入到容错率为零的高精密工业流水线时，它们之间究竟应该以何种协议进行“零信任”的协同与确权？**

The **Nooxus-AI Team** established this open-source research community dedicated to answering an ultimate proposition: **When AI agents cross enterprise intranet firewalls and international borders to penetrate high-precision industrial assembly lines with zero fault tolerance, what kind of protocol should be used for "Zero-Trust" collaboration and authorization among them?**

我们坚信，下一代 AI 互联网传递的不再是简单的 API 数据，而是**“理智与确权凭证（NOO - Nous Node）”**。

We firmly believe that the next-generation AI Internet will no longer transmit simple API data, but **"Reasoning and Authorization Credentials (NOO - Nous Node)"**.

**[EN]** Our research is deeply inspired by **Andrej Karpathy's** (former Director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI) vision of the **"LLM OS."** Karpathy foresaw the Large Language Model evolving into a central CPU that manages processes, memory, and peripherals.

**[CN]** Nooxus-AI 在此基础上，进一步探索了该构想在物理工业界的“缺失环节”：**确定性物理内核（Chancery）**。在此，我们向所有在 AI、操作系统及分布式系统领域做出卓越贡献的前辈研究者表示诚挚的敬意。

**[EN]** Building upon this foundation, Nooxus-AI explores the "missing link" of this vision in the physical industrial world: the **Deterministic Physical Kernel (Chancery)**. We express our sincere gratitude and tribute to the pioneering researchers in AI, Operating Systems, and Distributed Systems who paved the way.

### 📌 Project 01: LLM-OS 工业级确权中枢内核

### 📌 Project 01: LLM-OS Industrial-Grade Authorization Hub Kernel

本子项目是 AI 互联网理论在**“工业制造与具身智能”**场景下的首个物理落地架构研究。它推演了如何不推翻现有传统工业（如博世、西门子）的老旧体系，通过部署“旁路确权网关”，让大模型安全地指挥物理产线。

This sub-project is the first physical implementation architecture research of AI Internet theory in the scenarios of **"Industrial Manufacturing and Embodied AI"**. It deduces how to safely command physical production lines with large models by deploying "bypass authorization gateways" without overturning the old systems of existing traditional industries (e.g., Bosch, Siemens).

### 📌 Project 02: SGSC 全球供应链智能路由协同 (⏳ *Drafting*)

### 📌 Project 02: SGSC Global Supply Chain Intelligent Routing Collaboration (⏳ *Drafting*)

本研究旨在探讨如何利用 NOO 协议，将割裂的跨境采购、合规验证、跨国结算整合为一条由 AI 智能体自动谈判、确权与执行的单向流动链条。

This research aims to explore how to use the NOO protocol to integrate fragmented cross-border procurement, compliance verification, and international settlement into a one-way flow chain automatically negotiated, authorized, and executed by AI agents.

### 📌 Project 03: NOO (Nous Node) 本体论与密码学边界 (⏳ *Drafting*)

### 📌 Project 03: NOO (Nous Node) Ontology and Cryptographic Boundaries (⏳ *Drafting*)

本研究深入探讨“理智节点 (NOO)”的设计哲学，研究如何将 Web3 的不可篡改性与边缘计算的极低时延在数据结构层进行完美融合。

This research deeply explores the design philosophy of "Nous Node (NOO)", studying how to perfectly integrate the immutability of Web3 and the extremely low latency of edge computing at the data structure layer.
