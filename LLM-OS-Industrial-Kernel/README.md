## 📜 研究宣言 / The Manifesto

**[CN]** 随着具身智能（Embodied AI）的爆发，硅谷前沿共识已将 LLM 视作下一代计算平台的“操作系统 CPU”。然而，现有的多智能体研究大多脱离了物理世界对**绝对安全、低延迟、强确定性**的硬性要求。**Nooxus-AI** 致力于构建一套“物理确权机”，确保 AI 智能体在 1ms 级别的物理时延内，实现神经符号级的硬件状态闭锁。

**[EN]** As Embodied AI explodes, Silicon Valley consensus regards LLM as the "Operating System CPU". However, existing multi-agent research often lacks the **absolute safety, low latency, and deterministic** requirements of the physical world. **Nooxus-AI** is committed to building a "Physical Authorization Machine" to ensure AI agents achieve neuro-symbolic hardware state-locking within sub-1ms latency.
